How to verify if a broker is legitimate

1. Check financial regulator databases

Use trusted regulators: FCA (UK), CySEC (EU), ASIC (AU), NFA/SEC (US), etc. to check if afterprime.com is listed or blacklisted.

2. Review registration details

Check the broker’s stated legal name, address, and license number. Verify it matches official company registries.

3. Search independent forums

Use Trustpilot, Forex Peace Army, Reddit, or Quora to see what other traders report.

4. Use small deposits only

Never deposit large amounts into unverified platforms. Try a small test withdrawal first.
